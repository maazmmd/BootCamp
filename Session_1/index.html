Creativity

What is Creativity? Let's define creativity. The creative process is the act of making new connections between old ideas or recognizing relationships between concepts. Creative thinking is not about generating something new from a blank slate, but rather about taking what is already present and combining those bits and pieces in a way that has not been done previously. While being creative isn't easy, nearly all great ideas follow a similar creative process.
